### Address nomenclatura

- Geography
	- GeoIP_XXXX (Ex. GeoIP_Brasil)
- WAN
	- IP
		- IP_WAN_<Name_server>_<IP/Mask> (Ex. IP_WAN_sped_servers_200.198.239.21/32)
	- SN
		- SN_WAN_<Name_server>_<IP/Mask> (Ex. SN_WAN_sn_receitanet1_161.148.0.0/16)
- LAN
	- IP
		- ip_<interface>_<Name_server>_<IP/Mask> (Ex. ip_dmz_VCPPROXYFLUIG_172.16.12.36/32)
	- SN
		- sn_<interface>_<IP/Mask> (Ex. sn_dmz_172.16.12.0/22)

- FQDN
	- LAN
		- FQDN_<interface>_<FQDN> (FQDN_inside_VCP025968)
	- WAN
		- FQDN_WAN_<FQDN> (FQDN_WAN_viracopos.com)

- Group
	- GRP_<interface>_<serviço> (GRP_SERVERFARM_INTERNET_SERVIDORES)
